# Reflection – Balancing Stakeholder Needs

During the development of the Student Grade Management System requirements, several challenges were encountered when balancing the needs of different stakeholders.

One challenge was ensuring that the system remains simple for lecturers to use while still meeting the administrative requirements of the university. Lecturers require a system that allows quick and easy grade entry, while administrators need accurate records and reporting capabilities.

Another challenge was addressing both usability and security requirements. While students and lecturers need easy access to information, the system must also ensure that academic records are stored securely and protected from unauthorized access.

Additionally, the needs of IT support staff and system developers had to be considered. The system must be maintainable and stable so that it can be updated and supported without excessive technical difficulties.

Overall, balancing these stakeholder needs required careful consideration of system functionality, security, usability, and maintainability. The final requirements aim to create a system that supports academic staff while ensuring reliable and secure management of student grade data.
